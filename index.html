ðŸ’¡ Professional Trading Tips

Master these risk management principles to protect your capital and maximize long-term success

1-2% Rule
Never risk more than 1-2% of your account on a single trade. This protects you from devastating losses.
Position Sizing
Calculate position size based on your stop loss distance, not your desired profit. Risk comes first.
Risk:Reward Ratio
Aim for at least 1:2 risk-reward ratio. This means your potential profit should be twice your risk.
Stop Loss Placement
Place stop losses based on technical levels, not arbitrary percentages. Support/resistance levels work best.

Remember: Proper risk management is the foundation of successful trading. Use this calculator before every trade.
